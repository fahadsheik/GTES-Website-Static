Our Services

What We Offer

At Global Twinkle Engineering Services, we deliver premier engineering solutions by combining deep industry expertise with the most advanced technology. Our dedicated teams provide exceptionally detailed drawings, robust designs, and accurate modeling to ensure our clients' projects are executed with precision and efficiency. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and consistency in every project we undertake.

Structural Steel Detailing

We provide the highest quality drawings and 3D Models to our fabricators and detailing firms, using the most advanced structural steel detailing software, Tekla Structures. Our process ensures every component is meticulously detailed for flawless fabrication and assembly.

Our Detailing Deliverables Include:

Fabrication Drawings

Comprehensive shop drawings for manufacturing every steel component.

Erection Drawings

Clear and precise plans for on-site assembly and construction.

Single Part Drawings

Individual drawings for each unique part.

Advanced Bill of Materials (ABM)

Detailed lists of all materials required for the project.

Customised Reports

Project-specific reports tailored to client needs.

BIM File

A complete Building Information Model for integrated project management.

Weight Summaries

Accurate calculations of component and overall structure weight.

Bolt Summaries & Bolt Assembly Lists

Detailed lists for all required fasteners.

Weld Numbering / Weld Mapping

Systematic plans for all welding requirements.

NC Files

Data files for CNC (Computer Numerical Control) fabrication machinery.

KISS Files

Data interchange files for seamless software compatibility.

Structural Design & Analysis

Our design team provides the highest quality of structural design and 3D Models for fabricators and firms. We utilize leading structural steel design software like Staad Pro, Limcon3, and RAM Connection to deliver safe, efficient, and fully optimized engineering solutions.

Our Design Deliverables Include:

Staad Model

A complete structural model created in Staad Pro for analysis.

Load Analysis

Comprehensive analysis considering various loads and load combinations.

Structural Calculation & Design Reports

Detailed reports justifying the final design.

Primary & Secondary Structure Design

Complete design of all main and supporting structural elements.

Foundation Design

Robust foundation and anchor design plans.

Quantity Estimation (MTO)

Precise material take-offs for accurate budgeting.

Connection Design

Detailed calculations and reports for all structural connections.

Rebar Detailing & Estimation

GTES has vast experience in delivering accurate and affordable Rebar detailing services. We focus on providing the highest levels of quality and consistency for all reinforced concrete structures.

Our Rebar Services Include:

Rebar Concrete & Field Placement Shop Drawings

Detailed drawings for rebar fabrication and on-site placement.

Bar Bending Schedules (BBS)

Comprehensive schedules detailing the shape, size, and quantity of every rebar.

Foundation Drawings

Detailed foundation reinforcement plans.

Caisson & Piling Drawings

Specialized drawings for deep foundation systems.

Masonry Wall Drawings

Reinforcement details for masonry structures.

As-Built Drawings

Final documentation of completed construction.

Retaining Wall Drawings

Specialized reinforcement for retaining structures.

Civil Works Drawings

Infrastructure and civil engineering reinforcement plans.

Slab Detailing

Comprehensive slab reinforcement specifications.
