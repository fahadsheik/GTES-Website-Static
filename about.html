About GTES

Excellence in structural steel detailing and engineering solutions

Precision and Reliability in Steel Detailing

Welcome to Global Twinkle Engineering Services (GTES), a leading specialist in structural steel detailing. Founded on the principles of accuracy, efficiency, and unwavering quality, we partner with fabricators, engineers, and construction firms to bring complex structures to life. Our team leverages cutting-edge technology and deep industry experience to translate intricate designs into clear, precise, and actionable shop drawings and 3D models, ensuring seamless project execution from start to finish.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted and reliable partner in structural steel detailing by delivering exceptional quality, precision, and innovation in every project. We are committed to supporting our clients' success through accurate technical drawings, efficient project delivery, and unparalleled customer service.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as the premier provider of structural steel detailing services, setting industry standards for quality, innovation, and client satisfaction. We envision a future where our expertise enables the construction of safer, more efficient, and architecturally stunning structures worldwide.
